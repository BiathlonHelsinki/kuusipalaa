Kuusi Palaa closes on 30 June

The Kuusi Palaa project will end after only one season. The space will remain open for events & free use as normal, until the end of June. A cooperative annual/final meeting will be announced soon and held in July, during which time the cooperative will be dissolved and leftover money (after closing costs) returned.
